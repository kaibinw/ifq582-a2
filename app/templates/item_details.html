About this Item

A deeply moving oral history recording with Elder Aunty Doris Bancroft of the Yuggera people, sharing her childhood memories and generational stories of life along the Maiwar (Brisbane River). Aunty Doris brings the river corridor to life as she details traditional fishing customs, the seasonal movements of her ancestors, and the enduring community gathering spaces that map the water’s edge.

Cultural Note: This item has been reviewed and approved for public access by the Elder Advisory Council. It does not contain sacred or restricted knowledge.
⚠ This recording may reference deceased persons. Listener discretion is advised.
